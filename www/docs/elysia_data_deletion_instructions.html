Data Deletion Instructions for Elysia BOT

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

1. Introduction

At EvolverAI, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from Elysia BOT ("the Service"), you can follow the instructions below. Please note that data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data:

  1. Log in to your account on the Elysia BOT platform.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings or Profile section.
  3. Select the option to "Delete Account" or "Request Data Deletion."
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm your request. You may be asked to provide a reason or verify your identity.

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at [support@evolverai.com] with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include your account details.

3. What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon confirmation of your request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address).
  • Usage history and interaction logs.
  • Any stored preferences or settings.

Note: Some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained for legal, security, or analytical purposes, as permitted by law.

4. Timeline for Deletion

We aim to process data deletion requests within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

5. Additional Information

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact us at [support@evolverai.com]. For more details, refer to our Privacy Policy.
