Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

TL;DR: Love Timeline stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Your dating history is yours alone.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Love Timeline ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Love Timeline is a personal journal application designed to help users keep track of their dating history. We understand the sensitive nature of this data and have designed the App with privacy as a core principle.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not collect names, photos, or any data you enter into the App
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not access your contacts without your explicit action
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or ad networks

3. Data Storage

All data you enter into Love Timeline — including names, photos, notes, dates, and any other information — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

4. Photos and Camera Access

The App may request access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to add photos to your entries. These photos are stored locally on your device within the App's private storage. They are not uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any external service.

5. Contacts Access

The App offers an optional feature to link an entry to a phone contact. This requires your explicit permission. When you use this feature, the App reads only the specific contact you select. It does not scan, copy, or transmit your contact list.

6. Biometric Data

The App offers optional biometric authentication (fingerprint, Face ID) as a security feature. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system. The App never accesses, stores, or transmits biometric data. We only receive a success/failure response from the operating system.

7. In-App Purchases

Love Timeline offers optional premium features through in-app subscriptions. All purchase transactions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy or Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle purchase data.

8. Children's Privacy

Love Timeline is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are under 17, please do not use this App.

9. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. The App provides optional security features including PIN protection and biometric authentication to prevent unauthorized access to your data. Since all data is stored locally, the security of your data also depends on the security of your device.

10. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Delete individual entries from within the App
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • Use the "Delete All Data" option in the App's Settings

11. Third-Party Services

The App contains optional links to external social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) that you may enter. When you tap these links, you will be directed to the respective third-party website or app. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

13. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, traditional data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) are exercised directly by you through the App itself. You have complete control over all data stored in the App.
