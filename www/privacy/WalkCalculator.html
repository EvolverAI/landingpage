Data We Collect

Personal Health & Fitness Data

The App collects the following data that you provide directly:

  • Age, sex, height, weight, and target weight
  • Walking preferences (intensity, frequency, schedule)
  • Diet preferences (meals per day, fasting type, water intake goal)
  • Weight log entries
  • Walk session records (start time, end time, duration, steps, distance, calories)

Health Platform Data

With your explicit permission, the App reads the following data from Apple HealthKit (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android):

  • Step count
  • Walking/running distance
  • Body weight

The App requests read-only access to this data. We do not write data to HealthKit or Health Connect.

Notification Preferences

If you enable reminders, the App stores your notification preferences (times, intervals) locally on your device.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. We use:

  • SharedPreferences for user profile and settings
  • SQLite database for weight history and walk sessions

We do not transmit, upload, or share any of your data to any server, cloud service, or third party. There is no user account, no analytics, no advertising SDK, and no network communication of personal data.

Data Export

The App allows you to export your data voluntarily:

  • Walking plan as PDF
  • Weight history as CSV
  • Walk sessions as CSV

These exports are generated locally and shared only through your device's share functionality (e.g., email, messaging). We have no access to exported files.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party integrations are:

  • Apple HealthKit (iOS) — read-only access to steps and distance, with your permission
  • Google Health Connect (Android) — read-only access to steps and distance, with your permission

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it persists until you:

  • Clear the app's data
  • Uninstall the App
  • Reset your device

We have no ability to access or delete your data remotely.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

Since all data remains on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete all data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the App.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and Switzerland, you have rights under the GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Since no data leaves your device, these rights are inherently fulfilled.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
